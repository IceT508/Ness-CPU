About Me

Hello! My name is Ness and ever since I was a kid, I loved computers. My dad also had a computer repair shop when I was young. His commitment to the job sparked my love for computers today. I graduated from Virginia Tech in 2008. I have a bachelor’s degree in computer hardware engineering and I’m currently going for my masters. My love for computers has led to me to opening my own computer hardware store. This gives me the opportunity to learn more about computers and meet the wonderful people of Northern Virginia.

Our mission

At Ness CPU, customers always come first. When my father ran his computer repair shop, customers were always a priority. As owner of Ness CPU, I’m keeping the same mission statement as my father. We have a team of highly skilled computer repair technicians who have a professional background in computer repair. They pursuit to make you as happy as possible. Not only do we offer the fastest service in Virginia at a fair price, but we also have the best warranty there is. If you have ANY problem with our repairs, not only will we refund the original amount, but we will repair it for FREE. That’s the NESS way.
