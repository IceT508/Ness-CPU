Welcome to Ness' CPU!

Building, repairing, maintaining, replacing, ordering, recommendations, and more!

Ness CPU is a locally owned computer repair shop that is located in Northern Virginia in McConnellsburg. We fix all types of products. From Macbooks to Laptops, you name it, we can fix it! We have been serving the computer repair needs of NOVO and surrounding cities since we moved there in 2019. We offer everything from virus removal to severe damage repairs on laptops, desktops, personal, and business computers. Some of the services we provide include laptop screen repair, liquid damage repair, broken hinge repair, no power issues, broken charging port repair, Macbook repair, virus removal, data recovery, website design, and more. Since we are a small company, we offer the best prices in computer repair. All computer brands and models are welcomed including, but not limited to Apple, Dell, Asus, Acer, Lenovo, Samsung, Sony, Toshiba, Alienware, Chromebook, Fujitsu, HP, MSI, and many more. Our employees have years of experience in the computer repair industry. We also have an enormous inventory of computer parts which guarantees that we have the parts your need. We can fix any type of computer from antiquated laptops to the most advanced gaming computers, we can fix it! We even offer services that build computers for you. We guarantee you'll be happy with your repair and offer 90 day warranty on all replacement parts. We are so certain that our services are the best that we offer every customer a 90 day warranty on all services. If there is any problem, we fix it for FREE!
